Brand Philosophy

一瓶梅酒，不只為飲用而存在。
A Bottle of Plum Wine Exists for More Than Drinking.

對梅見而言，青梅酒不只是酒體本身，而是一種從原料出發、 經時間釀成，最終走進餐桌與人際分享的體驗總和。

For Meijian, plum wine is more than the liquid itself. It is a complete experience that begins with ingredients, evolves through time, and ultimately enters dining tables and shared moments between people.

我們希望把東方青梅的清香、節氣與文化感，轉化為更當代、 更易親近、也更適合全球市場理解的品牌語言。

We hope to transform the fragrance of oriental green plums, the rhythm of the seasons, and a sense of culture into a more contemporary, approachable, and globally understandable brand language.

Natural Ingredient

從一顆青梅開始，建立風味的起點。
From a Single Green Plum Begins the Origin of Flavor.

原料不是背景，而是品牌的出發點。 成熟青梅的酸、香、汁水與果肉狀態，共同決定了最終酒體的清爽度、純淨感與層次。

Ingredients are not a background detail, but the true starting point of the brand. The acidity, aroma, juiciness, and texture of ripe green plums together define the freshness, purity, and layered character of the final wine.

真正的風味，從原料開始被尊重。
True Flavor Begins with Respect for the Ingredient.

我們重視果實的真實性，也重視採摘時節。 青梅不是抽象的符號，而是梅酒一切香氣與口感的源頭。

We value the authenticity of the fruit and the timing of harvest. Green plums are not abstract symbols; they are the source of all aroma and flavor in plum wine.

青梅的鮮亮、微酸與自然果香，既是果實本身的自然表情， 也是品牌延伸風味層次的起點。

The brightness, gentle acidity, and natural fruit aroma of green plums are not only the fruit’s own expression, but also the beginning of the layered taste that defines the brand.

Craftsmanship

讓時間參與風味，讓工藝保持克制。
Let Time Shape the Flavor, and Let Craft Remain Restrained.

從挑選、處理、浸泡到熟成，工藝不是為了炫耀， 而是為了讓青梅原有的酸香被更穩定、更細緻地保留下來。

From selection and preparation to infusion and aging, craftsmanship is not meant to impress for its own sake, but to preserve the original acidity and fragrance of green plums in a more stable and refined way.

  • 重視果實狀態與處理節奏 / Respect the fruit’s condition and preparation rhythm
  • 讓青梅香氣與酒體慢慢融合 / Allow plum aroma and spirit to merge gradually
  • 以熟成建立更圓潤的層次感 / Build a rounder and more layered taste through aging

Dining & Sharing

從聚餐到微醺時刻，梅酒走進更日常的生活。
From Dining Gatherings to Gentle Moments, Plum Wine Enters Everyday Life.

青梅酒可以出現在朋友聚餐、家庭餐桌、節慶分享， 也能留給一個人的安靜片刻。它不必被過度定義， 卻能自然融入不同市場與不同生活方式之中。

Plum wine belongs at dinners with friends, family meals, festive sharing, and even quiet moments alone. It does not need to be overdefined; it can naturally fit into different markets and lifestyles.

Brand Culture

讓梅花、青梅與器物，一起說明品牌的氣質。
Let Plum Blossoms, Green Plums, and Objects Together Express the Spirit of the Brand.

品牌的感受不只來自味道，也來自視覺、節氣、器物與文化的共同作用。 梅見希望把這份來自東方的細膩，轉化為更當代的品牌語言。

The feeling of a brand is shaped not only by taste, but also by visuals, seasonal sensibility, objects, and culture. Meijian hopes to transform this oriental delicacy into a more contemporary brand language.

梅花與青梅，都是品牌敘事的一部分。
Plum Blossoms and Green Plums Are Both Part of the Brand Narrative.

梅花不只是裝飾元素，也象徵節氣、清雅與時間感。 當器物、花影與果實一起被放進品牌視覺之中， 品牌就不只呈現產品，也呈現一種更完整的東方生活方式美感。

Plum blossoms are more than decorative elements; they symbolize seasonality, elegance, and a sense of time. When objects, floral shadows, and fruit are placed together in the brand’s visual language, the brand presents not just products, but a fuller oriental lifestyle aesthetic.
